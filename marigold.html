Plot Description

The P&K Realtor’s newest addition is the P&K West Gate Marigold. Sprawling over 42 acres, it connects the Athipalayam- Saravanampatti Road and Chinna Mettupalayam Road- Saravanampatti Road. It also connects the NGGO Colony and Mettupalayam Road.

The P&K Marigold is just adjacent to Cognizant SEZ, IT Park that comprises SPHERIS, BOSCH, CTS, KGISL, DELL, YUGAM SOLUTION, HIROTECH, PEROT, KG METROPOLIS, and PLATINA are located. It also meets every fundamental amenity you look for in a premium housing plot.

The infrastructure includes excellent concrete with a steel drainage system, electrical posts with streetlights, fully developed parks, and rigid compound walls. The location has a magnificent view, absolute privacy, and a lot of greenery for fun, fitness, and leisure with proximity to major schools, colleges, banks, market, and bus stand.
